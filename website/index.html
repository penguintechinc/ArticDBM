Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a database?

Each managed database connection in your ArticDBM configuration counts as one database, regardless of size or usage.

Can I mix Community and Enterprise?

Yes! You can upgrade individual databases to Enterprise features while keeping others on Community edition.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for organizations with 10+ databases. Contact sales for custom pricing.

What threat intelligence feeds are supported?

Enterprise supports unlimited STIX/TAXII feeds, OpenIOC files, MISP integration, and custom threat feeds.

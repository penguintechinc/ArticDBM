Frequently Asked Questions

What databases does ArticDBM support?

ArticDBM supports MySQL, PostgreSQL, Microsoft SQL Server, MongoDB, and Redis. The proxy automatically detects the protocol and routes connections appropriately.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade from Community to Enterprise at any time. Contact our sales team to discuss your needs and transition timeline.

What threat intelligence feeds are supported?

Enterprise plans support unlimited feeds from STIX/TAXII servers, OpenIOC files, MISP platforms, and custom threat intelligence sources through our API.

Is there a trial period for Enterprise?

Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial of Enterprise features. Contact sales to get started with your trial deployment.

What's included in Priority Support?

Priority support includes email support with 4-hour response time, phone support during business hours, and access to our technical team for implementation guidance.

Can I white-label ArticDBM?

Yes, the Embedded plan includes full white-label rights, source code access, and the ability to remove all ArticDBM branding and embed it in your own application.
